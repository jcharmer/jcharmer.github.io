How it works

You set the rules. CrewDriver runs the call-out.

CrewDriver knows your roster, your seniority rules, and your preferences. It sends exactly one offer at a time. Workers reply YES or NO. Every offer is logged. You see everything.

  • SMS only — no app required Workers already know how to reply to a text. No onboarding, no passwords.
  • Seniority and availability rules enforced Your rules run automatically. One mistake doesn't become a grievance.
  • Full audit trail, every shift Who was offered. Who declined. Who confirmed. Timestamped and searchable.
