Our design decisions

Built around protecting the worker.

Dispatch automation has a bad reputation because most tools treat crew like inventory. CrewDriver was designed with the opposite philosophy — workers confirm themselves, no one is force-placed, and the seniority rules protect the relationships you've built.

Workers Confirm themselves. No forced placement. Every YES is their own choice.
Seniority Enforced automatically. One mistake doesn't become a grievance.
Midnight Texts go out in your configured hours. No one gets a call at 2am.
Record Every offer, every decline, every confirmation. Timestamped. Searchable.
Cost $3 per confirmed placement. Pay for what gets booked, nothing else.
