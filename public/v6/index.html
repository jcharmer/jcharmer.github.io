Seniority protection

The call-out runs in order. Every time. Without exception.

Manually enforcing seniority across a 200-person roster is where mistakes happen. One missed call, one out-of-order offer, and you're dealing with a grievance before the shift even starts. CrewDriver runs your seniority list automatically — no manual checking, no exceptions, full audit trail.

Seniority order enforced automatically

CrewDriver works through your roster in seniority order, skipping unavailable members and moving to the next in line — exactly as your agreement requires.

Full audit trail for every offer

Every offer is logged with a timestamp and the seniority position of the worker. When a grievance comes up, you can answer it in 30 seconds.

Workers decline — never skipped

If a worker is unavailable or declines, they're recorded as offered and passed. Their seniority position is preserved for the next call-out.

Availability windows respected

Workers can set times they're unavailable. The system skips them and circles back when they're next in line for a shift they can take.

How it works

SMS-only. Workers already know how to use it.

01

You create the shift

Set the date, time, role, rate, and any special requirements. CrewDriver knows your seniority list and your rules.

02

Offers go out in order

One targeted text per worker, working down your seniority list. Not a blast — a methodical, compliant call-out.

03

Workers reply by text

YES to confirm. NO to decline. No app, no portal, no password. Just a reply — logged, timestamped, auditable.
