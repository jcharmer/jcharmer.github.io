A confirmed placement is when a worker replies YES to a shift offer and the shift is recorded as booked. If a worker is offered a shift but doesn't confirm — no charge. You pay only for successful, worker-confirmed bookings.
There's a $20/month minimum on Bronze and Silver. Beyond that, you pay $3.25 per confirmed placement — nothing else. If you have a slow month, your bill reflects that. Gold plans can be structured with volume commitments — talk to us.

We support rotational, non-rotational, multi-list (A/B/C/D), fewest-hours-first, and contract-procedure dispatch. Whichever your CBA requires, it's configurable per list.
