How It Works

Three steps.
Your crew.
Your rules.

  1. 01

    Add your crew

    Import your existing list. Workers get a single text — they reply YES to opt in. No app download. No account creation. Just SMS.

  2. 02

    Send the call-out

    Set the shift details. Offers go out in your order — seniority, rotation, preference. One offer per position at a time.

  3. 03

    Workers confirm by SMS

    They reply from any phone. No portal. No password. No app. The crew confirms themselves — and you're done.
