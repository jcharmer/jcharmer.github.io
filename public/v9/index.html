How It Works

Three texts. Shift filled.

No dispatcher touching every call. No apps. No portals. Just SMS and your roster.

01

You create the shift

Set the date, time, role, and how many people you need. CrewDriver builds the offer list from your roster — seniority rules, availability, and relationship history included.

02

Crew gets a text

Workers receive an SMS offer — no app, no login, no password. They reply YES or NO. If someone declines, the next person on the list gets the offer automatically.

"CREWDRIVER: Shift Sat Apr 19, 8am–4pm, Rogers Arena. Reply YES to confirm or NO to pass."
03

You get confirmation

Once enough crew confirm, you get notified. The shift is locked. You pay $3 per confirmed placement — nothing for declined offers or no-shows.
